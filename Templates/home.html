How We Did It


Step 1: Drone sends real-time video feed to the smart glass embedded helmets.
           With realtime monitoring of brain activities, we use the Muse Headband to convert the EEG data into a heatmap of brain activity, which            can then be analyzed to identify potential fear responses.

Step 2: Based on the video feed, data & directions is shown on the smart glass.
Custom designed exposure therapy solutions can be administered using VR technologies to alleviate mental health disorders.
